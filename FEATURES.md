# Tire Empire — Feature Overview

**Tire Empire** is a massively multiplayer business simulation where you build a tire empire from scratch — starting with $500 and a van, scaling to factories, stock exchanges, and global distribution.

## Core Loop
Source tires → Set prices → Sell via van/shops/online → Build reputation → Expand operations → Dominate the market

---

## Major Systems (18 Panels)

### Sourcing & Inventory
- **Scrap Yards & Garage Cleanouts** — cheap used tires to flip
- **Supplier Network** (Rep 8+) — 7+ wholesale suppliers with tiered volume discounts (up to 13% off)
- **18+ tire types** — from Junk Used to Luxury Touring to EV Premium
- **Storage progression** — Van (20) → Garage (80) → Warehouse (6,000) → Distribution Center (18,000)

### Retail & Shops (Rep 15+)
- Open shops in **100+ US cities**, each with unique demand/cost profiles
- Hire **Technicians, Sales Staff, Managers, Drivers, Pricing Analysts**
- Offer services: flat repair, tire balance, installation, nitrogen fill
- Per-shop **marketing campaigns** (flyers, radio, digital) and **customer loyalty** tracking
- Shop financing with 20% down payment

### Wholesale Distribution (Rep 25+)
- Sell bulk to fleet operators and dealers
- 6 volume tiers: Standard → Diamond (20% discount)
- Client management with contract negotiation

### E-Commerce (Rep 30+)
- Launch your own online tire store ($150K)
- Hire 10 specialized e-commerce staff (SEO, developers, CS, data analysts)
- 7 upgrades: Fitment Database, Mobile App, Review Platform, Photo Studio, Installer Network, Road Hazard Program, Live Chat
- Revenue tiers from Invisible to Dominant ($6M+)

### Manufacturing & Factory (Rep 75+)
- Build a tire factory ($5M) with 3 upgrade levels (up to 500 tires/day)
- **5 Tire Performance Attributes**: Grip, Durability, Comfort, Tread Life, Efficiency — computed from quality, R&D, and certifications
- **R&D Projects** ($500K-$1.2M): Ultra-Grip, Silent Ride, EV Optimized, Heavy-Duty Commercial, All-Terrain Elite
- **Certifications**: DOT, H/V Speed Rating, ISO 9001
- **Brand Reputation** system — quality + attributes drive sales multipliers

### Raw Material Supply Chain
- **Rubber Farm** (5,000 TC) — produce natural rubber, 3 upgrade levels (5-30 units/day)
- **Synthetic Lab** (3,000 TC + $300K) — weather-immune synthetic rubber, 3 levels (8-40 units/day)
- Reduce effective rubber costs, sell surplus at market price
- Vertical integration = cost advantage over competitors

### Stock Exchange — TESX (Rep 10+)
- Trade stocks in other players' companies
- **IPO system** — go public at Rep 40+ with $500K+ revenue
- Advanced features: **Margin Trading**, **Short Selling**, **Dark Pool**, Advanced Charting, Price Alerts
- 5 Sector ETFs (TIRE, RTIL, MFGX, WHSL, ECOM)
- Market crashes, bubbles, and black swan events
- Capital gains taxes (15-25%), wealth taxes, dividend system

### Banking (Rep 3+)
- Savings account at 4.2% annual interest
- 6 loan tiers: $5K @ 14% → $750K @ 5% (scaling with rep)

### Player Trading & Marketplace (Rep 10+/20+)
- Direct P2P tire-for-cash/TC trades
- Revenue sharing deals
- Player marketplace with buy/sell listings
- Shop sales (sell entire locations to other players)

### Flea Markets & Car Meets
- 12 flea markets (Fri-Sun, used tire premium)
- 10 car meets (summer seasonal, performance tire premium)

---

## Global Market Events
8 economy-wide events affecting ALL players:
- **Rubber Shortage** — costs +40%, production +20%
- **Port Strike** — supplier orders disabled
- **Winter Storm Warning** — winter tire demand +80%
- **Economic Boom** — all demand +25%
- **Steel Surplus** — costs -30%
- **Tire Safety Recall** — branded demand -30%, used +40%
- **EV Mandate** — EV demand +60%
- **Holiday Rush** — all demand +40%

~2% daily trigger chance, max 2 concurrent, displayed on dashboard.

---

## Multiplayer
- **Real-time competition** with human + AI players in shared economy
- **Player chat** with block/unblock
- **Leaderboards** — all-time wealth ranking + weekly tournaments with TC prizes
- **Profile viewing** and trade offers from leaderboard

## Progression
Rep 0 (Van) → Rep 8 (Suppliers) → Rep 10 (Exchange/Trade) → Rep 15 (Shops) → Rep 20 (Marketplace) → Rep 25 (Wholesale) → Rep 30 (E-Commerce) → Rep 50+ (Factory/Distribution) → Rep 75 (Full Manufacturing)

## Monetization
- **Premium/PRO** ($4.99/mo) — +10% bank interest, +8% foot traffic, exclusive supplier tires, gold badge, lower exchange fees
- **TireCoins** — earned via achievements & gameplay, spent on TESX features, cosmetics, factory upgrades
- **TC Storage Cap** system with 5 upgrade levels
- **Rewarded Ads** — watch for 50 TC (3x/day)
- **6 Cosmetics** — Gold Name, Neon Glow, VIP Dashboard, Premium Van, Celebration Effects, Elite Border

## Notifications
6 configurable notification categories:
- Global Market Events, Cash Reserve Warning (configurable threshold), TC Storage Alerts, Inventory Alerts, Loan Payment Reminders, Factory Production

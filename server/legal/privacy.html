This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how Tire Empire ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information in connection with the Tire Empire mobile application ("the Game"). By accessing or using the Game, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Policy.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several categories of information when you use the Game:

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account through Firebase Authentication, we may collect:

  • Email address associated with your authentication provider.
  • Display name or username you choose for your in-game profile.
  • Unique user identifier (UID) generated by Firebase Authentication.
  • Authentication provider data (e.g., Google account ID if you sign in with Google).

1.2 Game Data

As you play, we collect and store information related to your gameplay:

  • Game state: Company data, shop locations, inventory, staff, cash balance, TireCoins balance, reputation, and all other game progress.
  • Transaction history: In-game purchases, sales, stock exchange trades, and shop market transactions.
  • Leaderboard data: Rankings, scores, and competitive performance metrics.
  • Chat messages: Messages sent through the in-game chat system, including message content, timestamps, and sender information.

1.3 Device and Technical Information

We and our third-party partners (including advertising networks) may automatically collect:

  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage data: Session duration, feature usage patterns, crash reports, and performance data.
  • IP address: Collected for security, rate limiting, and general geographic analytics.
  • Advertising identifiers: Such as the IDFA (iOS) or GAID (Android), collected by our advertising partners for ad personalization.

1.4 Purchase Information

When you make in-app purchases, transaction processing is handled by the platform payment provider (Apple or Google) and RevenueCat. We receive:

  • Confirmation of purchase and subscription status.
  • Transaction identifiers.
  • Subscription expiration dates and renewal status.

We do not directly collect or store your payment card numbers, bank account details, or other financial payment credentials.

4. Children's Privacy

Tire Empire is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar international regulations:

  • We do not knowingly solicit or collect data from children under 13.
  • If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take immediate steps to delete that information from our servers.
  • If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@tireempiregame.com so we can take appropriate action.

Users between the ages of 13 and 18 may use the Game with the consent and supervision of a parent or legal guardian who agrees to be bound by our Terms of Service.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Game and its services. Specific retention periods include:

  • Account and game data: Retained for the lifetime of your account and for a reasonable period after account deletion to allow for account recovery requests.
  • Chat messages: Retained for moderation and safety purposes. Chat logs may be retained for up to 12 months after the message was sent.
  • Transaction records: Retained for up to 3 years for accounting, tax, and legal compliance purposes.
  • Server logs and technical data: Retained for up to 90 days for security and debugging purposes.

When data is no longer required for the purposes outlined in this Policy, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encrypted data transmission using TLS/SSL protocols.
  • Secure authentication through Firebase Authentication.
  • Access controls and authentication requirements for administrative systems.
  • Rate limiting and monitoring to detect and prevent abuse.
  • Regular security reviews and updates of our systems.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. You acknowledge and accept that you provide your information at your own risk.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information. We support the following rights for all users:

7.1 Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time by contacting us at support@tireempiregame.com. Upon receiving a verified deletion request:

  • Your account will be deactivated and your game data will be scheduled for deletion.
  • Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.
  • Anonymized or aggregated data that can no longer be associated with you may be retained for analytics purposes.
  • Active subscriptions should be cancelled through the respective app store before requesting account deletion.

7.2 Data Export

You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you. We will provide this information in a commonly used, machine-readable format (such as JSON) within 30 days of a verified request.

7.3 Additional Rights

Depending on your location, you may also have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Restrict or object to certain processing of your personal information.
  • Opt out of personalized advertising (through device settings or by contacting us).
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority if you believe your rights have been violated.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.
